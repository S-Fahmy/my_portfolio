IST Industrial Services and Technology website

IST is a company working in the industrial field and the main purpose of the website is to to display the company’s profile, information about their services, team introduction, contact informations and products.

MY ROLE:

I was responsible for the design and the development of the website.
I worked along with the Manager and the engineering team to achieve the design they needed to showcase their content, their work, the company’s goal and products.

Figma was used to design the UI and is built with html, css and javascript.

CHALLENGES:

As any other project this one had it’s challenges, one of them is that we have a page to build that showcases a product with multiple parts, and each part has many high resolution images and content that needed to be displayed.
So i decided to use an accordion like effect and and use much smaller resolution for the images thumbnails with an zoom box that loads the original images.
